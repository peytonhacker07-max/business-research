My Story

For more than 13 years, I have homeschooled my five children using a variety of educational approaches and curricula. Through this journey, I have gained firsthand experience helping students with different learning styles and educational needs, including children with dyslexia using Orton-Gillingham-based methods.

My educational philosophy combines structure and scheduling with hands-on learning, literature-based education, and outdoor experiences. Throughout the years, I have also taught extensively within homeschool co-ops and group learning environments, giving me experience working with both individual students and classroom settings.

My passion is helping families avoid frustration and confusion by finding the right curriculum, creating practical schedules, setting achievable goals, and building confidence in their homeschool journey.

Whether you're just getting started or need help solving a specific challenge, my goal is to provide personalized guidance that fits your family's unique needs.
