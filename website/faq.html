Frequently Asked Questions

What does a session actually look like?

Each session is a one-on-one video call (via Microsoft Teams) where we talk through your specific questions — curriculum, scheduling, challenges, whatever you need. You'll leave each call with a clear next step.

Do I need to know anything about homeschooling before booking?

Not at all. Many families I work with are brand new to homeschooling. We start wherever you are.

What if my child has a learning challenge like dyslexia?

I have personal experience navigating learning challenges, including dyslexia and approaches like Orton-Gillingham, and can help you find resources and strategies that fit your child.

Is this only for Tennessee families?

I specialize in Tennessee homeschool compliance, but families from anywhere are welcome — curriculum and scheduling advice applies no matter where you live.

How do I pay?

Payment is handled securely through Calendly at the time of booking. The Homeschool Success Package is a one-time $165 payment for all 3 sessions.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem — use the reschedule link in your confirmation email to pick a new time that works.
