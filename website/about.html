My Story

I've been a homeschooling mom for 13 years. I've homeschooled all 5 of my children through elementary and middle school, and along the way I've learned what works, what doesn't, and how to build a routine that fits a busy family.

I've sat at the kitchen table at 9pm trying to figure out which curriculum was actually right for my kid. I've rearranged the whole day three times trying to make a schedule that didn't fall apart by 10am. I've worked through learning challenges with my own children, including dyslexia, and learned firsthand what real support looks like — not just generic advice.

Now I'd love to share that experience and help other moms navigate their own homeschooling journey with more confidence and less stress.
